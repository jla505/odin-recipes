Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodle

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • Sugar: Two tablespoons of white sugar add subtle sweetness and enhance the flavor of the sauce.
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 1 can (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon fennel seeds
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 4 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 2 cups cottage cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package directions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Add the onion and garlic and cook for another 5 minutes.
  4. Add the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, water, sugar, basil, fennel seeds, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix together the Parmesan cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella cheese, cottage cheese, eggs, and parsley.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish. Place a layer of cooked lasagna noodles on top of the sauce. Spread one-third of the cheese mixture over the noodles. Repeat with another layer of sauce, noodles, and cheese. Continue layering until all ingredients are used up.
  7. Sprinkle the remaining 2 cups of mozzarella cheese over the top of the lasagna.
  8. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Let the lasagna rest for 10 minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy!
